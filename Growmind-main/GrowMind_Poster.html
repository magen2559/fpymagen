GrowMind

A Gamified Focus and Productivity App Using a Pomodoro Timer and a Virtual 3D Garden

Final Year Project | Faculty of Engineering, Built Environment, and Information Technology

Izz Bin Ismail, Ms Norizan Binti Johari

Abstract
GrowMind is a novel application addressing student distraction and focus. By integrating a Pomodoro timer with a reward system, users build and nurture a virtual 3D garden through focused study sessions. The positive reinforcement encourages sustained concentration and boosts productivity, gamifying the academic workflow and enhancing student outcomes.
Objectives
  • 1. Develop a Pomodoro-style focus timer for study sessions.
  • 2. Implement an immersive virtual 3D garden that evolves.
  • 3. Create a coin-based reward system for successful focus cycles.
  • 4. Enable user authentication and progress tracking.

Conclusion & Future Work
GrowMind successfully demonstrates the effectiveness of integrating gamification with focus tools. Students reported increased engagement and improved concentration. Future work will focus on introducing social features, advanced analytics, and cross-platform compatibility.

Commercialisation Potential

  • Explore freemium models with premium garden elements.
  • Institutional licensing for universities and study centers.
  • In-app purchases for specialized 3D assets.
  • Potential for data insights on student productivity.
